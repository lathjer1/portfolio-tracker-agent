# Investment Theses

This document explains *why* each non-core holding exists.
If a thesis breaks, the position gets reviewed regardless of allocation.

---

## AAPL
Why owned:
- Platform ecosystem with high switching costs
- Services margin expansion

What breaks the thesis:
- Structural margin compression
- Regulatory action that materially weakens ecosystem lock-in

What would justify adding:
- Market drawdown with fundamentals intact

---

## MSFT
Why owned:
- Enterprise software dominance
- Cloud + AI platform leverage

What breaks the thesis:
- Sustained loss of enterprise relevance
- Material margin erosion

What would justify adding:
- Temporary multiple compression without business deterioration

---

## KNSL
Why owned:
